Resources

In order to provide assistance that may not be found in our chatrooms we have provided these hotlines for your use if you deem them necessary.

    Battered Women and their Children 1-800-603-HELP

    If you are a victim of domestic violence and are looking for assistance of any sort please call this hotline.

    Crisis Pregnancy Hotline Number 1-800-67-BABY-6

    If you are pregnant and would like to explore your options contact this hotline.

    Deaf Hotline 1-800-799-4TTY

    If you are Deaf and in need of assistance of any sort or have any queries please contact this hotline.

    Drug Abuse National Helpline 1-800-662-4357

    If you deal with substance abuse and want to curb your addiction please this hotline.

    Eating Disorders Awareness and Prevention 1-800-931-2237

    If you deal with Anorexia nervosa or Bulimia please call this hotlines.

    Family Violence Prevention Center 1-800-313-1310

    If you have seen or experienced ongoing verbal or physical Violence in your home please.

    Gay and Lesbian National Hotline 1-888-843-4564

    Members of the LGBTQ+ community seeking assistance of ANY form please call this hotline.

    Girls and Boys Town 1-800-448-3000

    Non-profit geared towards caring for children and families.

    Hearing Impaired 1-800-448-1833

    If you have a hearing impairment and need assistance please contact this hotline.

    National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-SAFE

    If you deal with physical or verbal abuse at home and are looking for a way out please call this hotline.

    National Runaway Safe line 1-800-RUNAWAY (786-2929)

    Aimed towards runaway and homeless teens this hotline offers resources to them as well as their families.

    National Sexual Assault Hotline 1-800-656-HOPE (4673)

    Any form of Sexual abuse (unwanted sexual contact) is unacceptable if you have faced sexual abuse or are still facing sexual abuse please call this hotline.

    Suicide prevention hotline: 1-800-273-8255

    Hotline geared towards the prevention of suicides. If you have experienced suicidal thoughts or actions or are seriously contemplating suicide give this hotline a called

    Teen Hope Line 1-800-394-HOPE

    If you are a teen in unfortunate circumstances and would like assistance please contact this line.

    Youth Crisis Hotline 1-800-448-4663

    If you are a youth in need of any form of assistance please contact this hotline
